# Operator Aliases

In addition to the symbols found below,
all operators can be used in their word form case insensitively ("aNd" is just as valid as using "&").

## AND

- `∧`
- `&`
- `&&`
- `*`

## OR

- `∨` (`U+2228`, not be confused with the latin v)
- `|`
- `||`
- `+`

## XOR

- `↮`
- `⊕`
- `⊻`
- `≢`
- `>=<`
- `>-<`
- `!=`
- `!==`
- `~=`
- `<>`
- `^`

## NOT

- `¬`
- `~`
- `!`

## IFF

- `⟷`
- `⇔`
- `≡`
- `<->`
- `<=>`
- `=`
- `==`
- `===`
- `XNOR`

## IFTHEN

- `→`
- `⇒`
- `⊃`
- `->`
- `=>`

## NAND

- `⊼`

## NOR

- `⊽`
